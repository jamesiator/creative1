 the Foreigner Project 

“The World is a Book...

...and those who do not travel read only a page.” That quote, attributed to St. Augustine, beatifully relates to our vision here at the Foreigner Project. Human life all over the world is rich and worth learning about, and we believe that the more one is exposed to life outside his or her own sphere of comfort, the greater that person's appreciation will be for peoples of other cultures as well as for the people and culture back home.

A Chain of Support

We are not a travel agency. We are not a vacation planner. We are not trip organizers. We campaign to encourage people to get out and live as a foreigner in another country, to really experience life and culture in another place. The bulk of our work is in offering professional support and advice to help those that want to live abroad and offering a lifeline to those already abroad that need help.

We offer help in areas such as:

  • Obtaining a passport
  • Applying for a visa
  • Obtaining student/work visas
  • Counseling for finding housing
  • Connecting with other expats
  • And more
